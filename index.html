ZENG LAB · Biostatistics

Welcome to the Zeng Lab in the Department of Biostatistics at Xuzhou Medical University. We develop biostatistical and statistical genetics methods for large-scale population cohorts. Our research focuses on causal inference (Mendelian randomization, mediation and sensitivity analyses), robust evidence triangulation and pleiotropy control, polygenic risk scoring and transfer learning for under‑represented populations, and integrative analysis of multi‑omics data (transcriptomics, proteomics, and spatial/single‑cell modalities). We apply these methods to cardiovascular disease, neurodegenerative disorders (e.g., Parkinson’s disease and ALS), and aging/dementia, often leveraging resources such as the UK Biobank to deliver reliable, clinically meaningful insights.
